# VITAMIN D3 (CHOLECALCIFEROL)

## What is it?
- Fat-soluble vitamin
- Chemical name: Cholecalciferol
- Source: Lanolin (sheep wool) OR Lichen (vegan)
- Type: Secosteroid (hormone-like)
- **Your choice: Vegan D3 from lichen**

## D3 vs D2:
| D3 (Cholecalciferol) | D2 (Ergocalciferol) |
|---------------------|---------------------|
| 3-5x MORE bioavailable | Less effective |
| Longer half-life (2-3 weeks) | Shorter (1-2 weeks) |
| More potent | Less potent |
| 90%+ of supplements use D3 | Rarely used |

## Key Functions:
- ✅ Bone Health (calcium absorption, bone strength)
- ✅ Immunity (activates T-cells, fights infection)
- ✅ Brain Health (mood regulation, cognitive function)
- ✅ Muscle Strength (protein synthesis, performance)
- ✅ Hormonal Balance (testosterone, thyroid)

## Deficiency Risks:
- Fatigue, bone pain, muscle weakness
- Depression, hair loss
- Osteoporosis, diabetes risk
- Common in India (skin pigmentation + indoor lifestyle)

## Your Dose:
- 400 IU (10 µm) ✅
- ICMR RDA: 5-15 µm ✅ PERFECT FIT
- Upper limit: 100 µm ✅ SAFE

# GUMMY MANUFACTURING PROCESS

## Steps:
1. **Prepare solution:** Water + pectin + sweeteners (heat 70-80°C)
2. **Add active ingredients:** KSM-66 + D3 + BioPerine (mix uniform)
3. **Add flavor & color:** Natural flavors/colors
4. **Add acid:** Citric/malic acid (pH for gelling)
5. **Deposition:** Pour into silicone molds (precise dosing)
6. **Cool & set:** 15-30 mins gelation
7. **Demold:** Remove, inspect
8. **Coating** (optional): Anti-stick coating
9. **Drying:** Reduce moisture, prevent stickiness
10. **Packaging:** Jar/tube + desiccant + seal

## Quality Control:
- Potency testing (all ingredients)
- Microbial testing (bacteria, yeast, mold)
- Heavy metal testing (critical for ashwagandha)
